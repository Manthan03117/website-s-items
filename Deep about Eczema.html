Eczema:

Details:

Eczema, also known as atopic dermatitis, is a chronic skin condition characterized by red, itchy, and inflamed skin.

Symptoms:

Symptoms can include itching, redness, dry skin, rash, and in severe cases, oozing blisters.

Causes:

The exact cause is not known, but it is believed to involve a combination of genetic and environmental factors, as well as an overactive immune response.
