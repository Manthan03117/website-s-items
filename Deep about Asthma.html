Asthma:

Details: Asthma is a chronic respiratory condition that causes airways to become inflamed and narrowed, making it difficult to breathe.

Symptoms: Common symptoms include wheezing, shortness of breath, coughing, and chest tightness.

Causes: Asthma can be triggered by various factors, including allergens, respiratory infections, exercise, and irritants. It's primarily an immune system and airway inflammation disorder.
