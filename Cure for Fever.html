Ayurveda recommends various natural remedies for fever, but it's essential to consult a qualified Ayurvedic practitioner for personalized guidance. Here's a general remedy: 1. Tulsi (Holy Basil): Tulsi leaves have anti-pyretic properties. Boil fresh tulsi leaves in water, and drink the decoction to reduce fever.
2. Ginger and Honey: Mix ginger juice and honey in equal proportions. Consume this mixture to reduce fever and boost your immune system.
3. Coriander Tea: Boil coriander seeds in water and drink the decoction. Coriander has cooling properties and can help reduce fever.
4. Fenugreek Seeds: Soak fenugreek seeds in water overnight and drink the water in the morning. Fenugreek can help lower body temperature.
5. Coconut Water: Coconut water is a natural electrolyte and can help with hydration during fever.
6. Rest and Hydration: Getting plenty of rest and staying hydrated is essential to help the body recover from fever.
